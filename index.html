ABOUT US

We love Learning for Fun!

NJ Design Hub is an online learning program offered by architect Nariman Jameel to equip young talents across industries and roles with the practical skills needed to succeed in the future of Architecture. NJ Design Hub is a three-level learning journey. It begins with exploring foundational skills, then moves to applying those skills through reflection, case studies, and group activities, and finally embedding new behaviors through ongoing peer and self learning, participatory approach and project based outcome. Our vision is to merge Art and Architecture with computation skills and elevate experiential learning through innovative and advanced technologies that aid in developing Multidiscplinary Designers.
